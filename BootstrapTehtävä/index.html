Train your Brain

On this site I will give you information about overall health and how to keep your body and mind healthy.
These days the importance of both, mental health and physical health are too often forgotten among people. The anxiety and deppression levels are at an all time high as well as obesity and cardiovascular diseases. This is because of many factors, here are some:

Social media

Yes, social media can be useful when it comes to recipes, tutorials or factual information, but much of the content on these platforms is just trash to "entertain" you. Many studies found out a strong connection between social media and an increased risk for:

  • Depression
  • Loneliness
  • Self-harm
  • Suicidal thoughts

    • Sleep

    These days over one third of people do not get the recommended amount of sleep. The recommended amount of sleep a night is 7-9 hours for adults. Some of the possible effects that chronic insomnia can cause:

  • Anxiety
  • Bipolar disorder
  • Depression
  • Diabetes
  • Heart attack

    • Exercise

    In many western countries only one fifth of the people exercise enough. Adults should aim for 150-300 minutes of moderate exercise or 75-150 minutes of stressfull exercise each week. Without exercise you have a greater risk for:

  • Depression
  • Dementia
  • Diabetes
  • High blood pressure
  • Obesity
