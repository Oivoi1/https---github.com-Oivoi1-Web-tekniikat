What is meditation ?

The purpose of meditation is not to become a better or "new" person. It's about educating awareness and finding a healthier perspective on yourself. You are not trying to quench your thoughts or feelings. You will learn to observe and be with your emotions, without being condemned. After all, you begin to understand them. Maybe you want to start meditating because you want to be less reactive, to feel less stressed, to focus better, to get to know yourself better or just as a part of your broader plan for personal growth.
